2A Sample Day

What a day looks like

Every day is built around comfort, connection, and routine. Here's the general shape of a day — call 947-333-9448 for current hours and this month's activity calendar.

  1. 1

    Warm welcome & arrival

    A friendly greeting, morning refreshments, and time to settle in with familiar faces.

  2. 2

    Morning activities

    Gentle group exercise, games, music, crafts, and conversation — matched to each person's interests and abilities.

  3. 3

    Lunch together

    A nutritious midday meal with company. Tell us about dietary needs and preferences when you call.

  4. 4

    Rest & quiet time

    A calm stretch of the day for resting, reading, or one-on-one time with staff.

  5. 5

    Afternoon programs

    Social activities, celebrations, and an afternoon snack before the day winds down.

  6. 6

    Departure & updates

    A smooth hand-off home — with staff sharing how the day went, so caregivers always stay in the loop.

3Good to Know

The practical details families ask about

The everyday logistics matter as much as the care. Here's what to ask about when you call — we'll walk you through exactly what applies to your family.

Hours of operation

Weekday daytime hours designed around work schedules and caregiver routines. Call 947-333-9448 for current hours and availability.

Transportation

Getting there shouldn't be the hard part. Ask about transportation options for Genesee County and Flint area families when you schedule your tour.

Meals & snacks

Lunch and snacks are part of the day, served with good company. Tell us about dietary needs, preferences, and restrictions when you call.

Ways to pay

Options may include the Medicaid Waiver (through the Area Agency on Aging), respite arrangements, and private pay. We'll walk you through what applies to your family.

Staff tenure runs 15 to 28 years — your loved one sees the same familiar, trusted faces day after day, year after year.

6Adult Day Care questions families ask

Common questions

A few of the questions we hear most. For anything specific to your situation, reach out and we'll help.

See all FAQs →

Where is Adult Day Care available?

Adult Day Care services are offered in Genesee County and the Flint area only. Home Help caregiver guidance is available throughout the State of Michigan.

What happens during the day?

Your loved one receives supervised, supportive care with social activity and companionship in a safe environment, giving family caregivers dependable time during the day.

How is this connected to Home Help?

ACAI Home Help guides families through Home Help caregiver options statewide, and offers Adult Day Care locally in the Flint area. We help you understand which path — or combination — fits your family. Learn more on our Home Help page.

What are the hours, and is transportation available?

The center runs weekday daytime hours built around caregiver work schedules, and transportation options may be available for Genesee County families. Call 947-333-9448 for current hours, availability, and transportation details.

Can we try it before committing?

Yes — schedule a tour and ask about 2 free trial days. Your loved one can meet the team, join the activities, and experience a full day before your family decides anything.

What does it cost to ask?

Nothing. Requesting information and guidance is free. Call 947-333-9448 or request no-cost guidance to get started.
