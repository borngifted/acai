Trusted Caregiver Support · ACAI Home Help

Many families already have a daughter, son, spouse, relative, close friend, neighbor, or caregiver showing up every day. ACAI Home Help helps families understand how that trusted person may be able to continue providing care through the proper Michigan Home Help process.

  1. 1

    Understand eligibility

    We ask a few simple questions about Medicaid status, daily care needs, and who is already providing care.

  2. 2

    Explain enrollment steps

    We walk through what caregiver enrollment involves, including CHAMPS registration and screening.

  3. 3

    Organize next steps

    We help you put the paperwork and timeline in order, so nothing important gets missed.
