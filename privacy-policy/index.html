Privacy Policy

How ACAI Home Help collects, uses, and protects the information families share with us.

1Your Information, Handled with Care

Effective date: July 13, 2026 · Applies to ACAIHomeHelp.com, operated by Annuit Coeptis & Associates Inc. (“ACAI Home Help,” “we,” “us”).

Information we collect

When you use our eligibility check or contact forms, we collect the information you choose to share, which may include:

  • Your name and telephone number;
  • Your county and general Medicaid status;
  • Who needs care and who the caregiver would be; and
  • Your preferred callback time.

We do not ask for Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or detailed medical records through this website. Please do not submit that information through online forms.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to respond to your inquiry, follow up about Michigan Home Help, Adult Day Care, and related care options, and guide you through possible next steps. We do not sell your personal information.

Contact consent

By submitting a form on this website, you authorize ACAI Home Help to contact you by telephone, text message, or email regarding your inquiry. Consent is not a condition of receiving information or services. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of further contact at any time by telling us by phone at 947-333-9448 or by replying STOP to a text message.

How your information is transmitted and stored

Form submissions are delivered to our intake inbox through a third-party form delivery service acting on our behalf. Information is transmitted over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. We limit access to submitted information to staff who handle family inquiries.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate this website and receive inquiries, or when required by law. Final eligibility and authorized services are determined by MDHHS or the applicable program; if you choose to move forward, information you provide to those agencies is governed by their own policies.

Retention

We keep inquiry information only as long as needed to respond to you and to meet our business and legal obligations, then delete it.

Children

This website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Accessibility

We want every family to be able to use this website. If you have difficulty accessing any part of it, call 947-333-9448 and we will help you directly and work to fix the issue.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version on this page with a new effective date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Call ACAI Home Help at 947-333-9448.
