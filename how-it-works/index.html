The Process

From first call to care that’s paid for

Here is exactly what happens after you reach out — eight clear steps from your first questions to authorized, documented care, with ACAI guiding your family the whole way.

Call ACAI Today
1What Happens After You Call?

You choose the caregiver you trust. We help guide the enrollment process.

At ACAI Home Help, our role is to help guide your family through the Michigan Home Help process so your loved one can receive support at home — often from someone they already know and trust.

  1. 1

    You contact us

    We ask simple questions about Medicaid, daily care needs, who is currently providing care, and whether a trusted person wants to become the caregiver.

  2. 2

    We explain the Home Help Program

    We walk through common support areas such as bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility, eating, toileting, meals, laundry, light housekeeping, and other approved care needs.

  3. 3

    We help identify the caregiver

    A trusted family member, friend, neighbor, or current caregiver may be able to enroll if they meet the applicable requirements.

  4. 4

    Care needs are reviewed

    The loved one’s daily living and household support needs are reviewed. The appropriate state worker or program determines approved services and hours.

  5. 5

    Caregiver enrollment begins

    We help families understand provider enrollment steps, state systems, and documentation expectations.

  6. 6

    Approval and authorization

    Caregiver approval is required before payment. Approval is not automatic, and final decisions are made by the appropriate program or agency.

  7. 7

    Services begin

    Once approved, care is delivered according to authorized services, timekeeping rules, service verification, and documentation requirements.

  8. 8

    Ongoing support

    ACAI helps families stay organized, understand next steps, and keep care focused on helping the loved one remain safely at home.

2How ACAI Helps

Clear guidance before families move forward

The Home Help process can feel overwhelming. ACAI helps families understand what to ask, what to prepare, and how to keep the next step clear.

  • Understanding Home Help eligibility concepts.
  • Explaining caregiver enrollment steps.
  • Helping families organize the right information.
  • Guiding families through the questions to ask.
  • Clarifying the difference between family care, agency support, and Adult Day Care.
  • Providing follow-up so families are not left trying to figure everything out alone.

An important note on compliance

Final approval, authorized hours, and payment decisions are made by the appropriate program or agency. ACAI does not guarantee approval.

Our role is to help families move forward with confidence, dignity, and clear next steps.
